Our Story

Born in Maboneng, Raised by Tradition

Ubuntu Eats was founded in 2018 by chef Nandi Dlamini, inspired by her grandmother's recipes and the rich tapestry of pan-African culinary traditions. What started as one restaurant has grown into three Johannesburg locations — united by a simple belief: food is community.
